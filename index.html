Malaria Kills.

  • Malaria is a preventable disease which affects almost half of the world's population and causes over 660,000 deaths each year
  • it is caused when a female anopheles mosquito carrying the malaria protozoan bites you, through which malaria enters into your blood stream
  • over 3000 children die of malaria everyday where 90% of the deaths occur in sub-Saharan Africa. This disease exists in 109 countries of the world. India is also affected every year especially during the rainy season when the mosquitos breed.
  • Symptoms include fever, chills and headache
  • To know more about malaria click here (wikipedia)
  • Like mentioned, it is preventable so here are 3 ways you can prevent malaria:
    1. Sleep under a mosquito net
    2. Try to avoid having stagnant water around and spray kerosene on it to avoid having mosquitos breed
    3. If the situation is very bad, apply mosquito repellants and other products to kill mosquitos
